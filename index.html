About Us

Building Faith,
Strengthening Families

Family Church is an evangelical Christian community founded by Pastor Marcos Augusto and his wife Sheila Augusto in the heart of Stamford, Connecticut. We are passionate about preaching the Word of God and creating an environment where everyone can experience His love.

Our services are held in Portuguese with live English translation, because we believe God can serve anyone regardless of language. Whether you speak English or Portuguese, there is a place for you here. Come as you are — you are family.
